## Usage Notes

- **Who should evaluate**: UX professionals, designers, or trained evaluators
- **How many evaluators**: 3-5 evaluators (finds 75-90% of issues)
- **When to use**: Early in design, after major changes, before usability testing
- **How long**: 1-2 hours per evaluator
- **Follow-up**: Combine findings from all evaluators, prioritize based on severity and frequency
