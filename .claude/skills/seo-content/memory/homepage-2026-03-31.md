# SEO Content Session: Homepage

Date: 2026-03-31

## Brief

- **Target**: Full Homepage (all sections)
- **Audience**: All segments — startups, enterprise, CTOs, business owners
- **Conversion goal**: Contact form submission
- **Tone**: Enterprise Professional + Technical Authority blend
- **Competitors referenced**: Vercel, Supabase, Railway (style inspiration)
- **Language**: English

## Keyword Strategy

- **Primary**: custom software development studio, software development company
- **Secondary**: AI development services, Web3 development, fullstack development agency, Next.js development, enterprise software development
- **Long-tail**: cost, MVP timeline, technologies, startups vs enterprise, choosing partner, development process

## Final Choice

- **H1**: Custom Software Development Studio — Web, AI & Web3
- **Structure**: D (SEO-dense) + B (why-us before services) + A (technical tone)
- **Tone**: Technical Authority meets Enterprise Professional
- **CTA**: "Start Your Project" (primary), "See Our Work" (secondary)
- **Meta title**: Custom Software Development Studio — Web, AI & Web3 | CHTZ-Tech
- **Meta description**: Senior engineers building production-grade web apps, AI integrations, and Web3 infrastructure. Type-safe code, proven process. Start your project today.

## Naming Decision

- **Name chosen**: CHTZ-Tech
