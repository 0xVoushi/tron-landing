# Brand Values & Positioning

> **Status**: Draft — refine after naming and first content sessions.

## Core Values

### 1. Technical Excellence
We ship production-grade code, not prototypes. Every project meets the same standard: type-safe, tested, performant, accessible.

### 2. Full Ownership
We don't hand off half-finished work. From architecture to deployment, from first pixel to last migration — we own the outcome.

### 3. Modern Stack, Pragmatic Choices
We use cutting-edge technology when it serves the product, not for the sake of novelty. Next.js over a custom framework. Tailwind over CSS-in-JS. The right tool for the job.

### 4. Speed Without Shortcuts
Fast delivery doesn't mean cutting corners. We optimize both velocity and quality — CI/CD from day one, automated testing, performance budgets baked in.

### 5. Honest Communication
No hidden complexity, no surprise invoices, no "that's out of scope" six weeks in. We scope clearly, communicate early, and deliver on promises.

## Positioning Statement

> **CHTZ-Tech** is a development studio for teams that need senior engineering capacity without the overhead. We build fast, performant web products across the modern stack — from Next.js marketing sites to AI-powered platforms and Web3 infrastructure.

## Competitive Differentiators

1. **Rare breadth**: AI + Web3 + Fullstack in one team (most agencies specialize in one)
2. **Senior-only**: No junior developers learning on your project
3. **Performance-first**: Every site ships with 95+ Lighthouse, < 100KB JS budget
4. **Full lifecycle**: We don't just build — we architect, deploy, monitor, iterate
5. **Speed**: Small team = fast decisions, no layers of project management
